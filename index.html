Our Story

Welcome to El Café Mocha, where every cup tells a story. Founded in 2024, our café was born from a passionate collaboration of coffee farmers nestled in the rich, fertile highlands of Mount Kenya. Our journey began with a shared dream: to bring the finest, sustainably grown coffee beans from our farms directly to your cup.

At El Café Mocha, we believe that great coffee isn’t just about the brew – it’s about the people, the culture, and the connection it fosters. With each sip, you're not just tasting coffee; you're savoring the rich heritage of our land and the hard work of dedicated farmers. Come, be a part of our story, and discover the magic of truly exceptional coffee.
